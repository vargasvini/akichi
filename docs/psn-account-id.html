Find your PSN Account ID

akichi needs your PSN Account ID to register with your console — this is not your username / online ID. It’s a numeric identifier tied to your PlayStation Network account.

Easiest — recommended

Use “Sign in with PSN”

  1. On the Register Console screen in akichi, tap “Sign in with PSN”.
  2. Log in with your PlayStation Network account in the page that opens.
  3. akichi reads your Account ID and fills it in automatically. Done!

Your login goes straight to Sony — akichi only receives the resulting Account ID, and nothing is stored on any server.

Manual entry

If you already have your account identifier, you can type it in the PSN Account ID field instead — akichi accepts either form:

  • 19-digit account number — a long decimal number (e.g. 1234567890123456789). akichi converts it for you.
  • Base64 Account ID — the 8-byte value encoded in Base64 (ends with =, e.g. AbCdEfGhIjk=).

Both point to the same account — use whichever one you have.

Heads up: the Account ID identifies your PSN account — treat it like personal info and don’t share it publicly. It is not your password and can’t be used to log in, but there’s no reason to post it.
