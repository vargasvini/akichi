akichi

Remote Play for PS5 and PS4 on Fire TV & Android TV — a modern chiaki fork, TV-first, with a Fire TV DualSense controller fix.

Download the latest APK

Install on Fire TV (Downloader)

Open the Downloader app and search akichi (or chiaki / remote ps5) to find this page, or enter the Downloader Code: (to be registered). Then install the APK.

What it does

Stream your PlayStation 5 or PlayStation 4 to your TV box and play with a controller, over your local network. Fixes the scrambled DualSense button mapping that Fire OS causes, and updates itself in-app.
